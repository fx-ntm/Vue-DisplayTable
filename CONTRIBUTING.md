## Ideas & Feedbacks
I'm very glad to hear any ideas or feedbacks about my code. You can feel free to share them by [creating an issue on the Issues page](https://github.com/fx-ntm/Vue-DisplayTable/issues) with the appropriate flair. 
I'm unfortunately busy on weekdays due to work and exams, but when I have free time, I'll definitely take a look and improve the code with your feedback! :D

## Adding your code
If you want to contribute by adding your code, please [create a pull request](https://github.com/fx-ntm/vue-displaytable/compare) with the list of changes you've done and I'll check it out!
